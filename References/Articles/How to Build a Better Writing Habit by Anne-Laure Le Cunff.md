### id944836573

> **1. Commit to a schedule.** Make a simple pact in this format:
> I will write [number of articles] in [time scale].

 * [View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k6tghdn58apsk69f48p42xy0)

### id944836643

> **2. Tell someone about your pact.** The best way I have found to achieve this level of consistency is to [commit to it publicly](https://nesslabs.com/learning-in-public).

 * [View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k6tgj1rwaa9rp7f278nyc89n)

### id944836810

> **3. Make time to write.** The last step is [shielding time](https://nesslabs.com/timeshielding-time-management) in your calendar for reading, research, and writing.

 * [View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k6tgjbpx860dxnywn9sq7f97)

### id944836919

> Now that you’ve made a pact, let’s break it down into smaller tasks. I call this the writing habit loop. Similar to any experiment, it consists in committing to an action, performing it, reflecting on the result, and implementing any changes during the next cycle.

 * [View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k6tgm58n0jnqnehb1qpja98q)

### id944837110

> Here is how I use the writing habit loop to write a new article every week:
> 1. **Generate ideas.** Jot down ideas whenever they pop up.

 * [View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k6tgmvyh02n73p3sr84fprqh)

### id944837198

> **Sit down and set a timer.** Because you have shielded your writing time, you just need to sit down and open a new document.

 * [View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k6tgn3v310bpjh9g9yh2j6hv)

### id944837267

> **Choose an idea.** Go through your list of ideas and pick a topic that feels alive and aligned with your current aspirations.

 * [View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k6tgnb02zmkabdt8x29ndwrd)

### id944837311

> **Create an outline.** Start by writing a few bullet points based on your initial thoughts about the topic.

 * [View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k6tgnhjgnea61g0v1zmh91za)

### id944837461

> **(optional) Conduct extra research.** Depending on the topic, you may be able to just write about the topic without any further help, but often it’s useful to look it up.

 * [View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k6tgp0gjafzrr38g78sgp2wh)

### id944837482

> **Re-read it once.** Yes, only once.

 * [View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k6tgpdzykvfd6z2g8zj7fmxy)

### id944837501

> **Hit publish.** Don’t look back. Bring that baby into the world.

 * [View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k6tgpkhpdygwgg6srwj4330r)

### id944837733

> **Share it.** Try to get as much feedback as you can. Don’t be shy. Share your article with friends, on social media, or through your mailing list.

 * [View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k6tgptadnx0b58rpetq4e86a)
