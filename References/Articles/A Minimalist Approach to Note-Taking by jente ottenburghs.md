### id943281927

> So, I stopped developing my elaborate note-taking system. Instead, I focus on taking targeted notes for particular projects or papers. And I don’t worry about remembering all the interesting facts when reading books on quantum mechanics or the downfall of the Roman republic. Reading books should be a fun activity, not a source of anxiety.
