# Lean Writing Method: How To Expand Short-Form Content Into Longer-Form Assets

## Highlights
### id941523854

> *You write...*
> *Which creates data points...*
> *Which reveals patterns (what works/what doesn’t)...*
> *Which shows you where there are opportunities to double-down...*
> *Which makes it easy to write the next thing...*
> *And the next thing...*
> *And so on...*
> That’s the game of [Digital Writing](https://www.ship30for30.com/post/what-is-digital-writing-7-rules-to-live-by) in a nutshell.

 * [View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k60qebpa7bmrhnp74hm2j30y)

> being a [prolific writer](https://www.ship30for30.com/post/how-to-become-a-prolific-writer)/creator really isn’t about being “brilliant” day after day after day. It’s about testing ideas in small ways, and then doubling-down and expanding those ideas when you see an opportunity to do so.

 * [View Highlight](https://read.readwise.io/read/01k60qetp2eyjxajfc9q59ngqc)
