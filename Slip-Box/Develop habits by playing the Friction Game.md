# Develop habits by playing the Friction Game

Our brain always choose to do the easiest thing at every moment. It’s designed to spend the less amount of energy on trivial things so it can have enough for survival.

In a modern world where we don’t need to survive anymore, this mechanisms work in ways we may not *love*. But they are like that.

> [!TIP]
> - Defining friction
> - What friction has to do with human behavior
> - Increasing friction for bad habits
> - Decreasing friction for good habits
