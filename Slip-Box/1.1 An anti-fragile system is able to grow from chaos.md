# An anti-fragile system is able to grow from chaos

Not only returning back to base (resilience), but getting better after it, benefiting from the stress, chaos and volatility of a situation. 

We see this kind of systems all of the time in stories, the main character who goes trough hardship just to getting better and stronger from it. It's the nature of stories and the human condition. Adapting, surviving and thriving.

> [!QUOTE]
>  Anti-fragility is a convex response to a stressor or source of harm, leading to a positive sensitivity to volatility (variability, stress or uncertainty). Likewise, fragility is defined as a concave sensitivity to stressors, leading to an increase in volatility.
