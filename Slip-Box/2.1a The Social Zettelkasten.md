# 2.1a The Social Zettelkasten

Zettelkastens and *Second Brains* are tools for thought. Most people use their knowledge systems in private.

The goal is that the notes talk to each other, externalizing your trains of thought.

But what if you can make your zettels not only to each other, but to an audience.

**The Social Zettelkasten is a system to learn, think and share better.**

A system to build an audience at the same time you 10x your learning and thinking
