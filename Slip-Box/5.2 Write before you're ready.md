# 5.2 Write before you’re ready

I used to read and consume an enormous amount of content before start writing.

This was counterproductive.

> Research is an amazing way of procrastination
> 
> – Tim Ferriss (I think)

Not feeling that I was ready yet to write the article just meant I wasn’t writing them. And I noticed the more I researched, the harder it became to start writing what I wanted to write in the first place.
