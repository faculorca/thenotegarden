# 7.4 Three reasons why startup hubs work

Based on the article [[Why Startup Hubs Work by Paul Graham]]

Startup Hubs are the reason why some places seem to commit “startupicide” and other places are fertile ground for them.

The truth is that startups are born poisoned, and there’s a few places that have the three things that spray the antidote.

1. Environment–startups need to be something cool to do.
	1. [[7.4a Startups need an ambitious environment]]
2. Chance–chance meetings with people that can help you as a founder
	1. [[7.4b Startup hubs 10X the surface of luck]]
3. Numbers–the amount of startup people around you powers everything.
	1. [[7.4c Startup hubs need a lot of people to work]]
