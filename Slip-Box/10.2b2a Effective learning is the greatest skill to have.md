# 10.2b2a Effective learning is the greatest skill to have

Learning effectively is the best skill to have nowadays.

The current state of things requiere us to reinvent ourselves every four to five years.

And the best way of doing that is by learning new things.

> [!QUOTE]
> It’s much more important today to be able to become an expert in a brand-new field in nine to twelve months than to have studied the “right” thing a long time ago.
> – Naval Ravikant
