# Specific actions to quick start your social media

This is how you start growing on social media (as a beginner):

- 3-5 DMs a day
- 2-3 posts a day
	- 21 posts weekly
- 1-2 threads, long posts or micro articles a week
- 10-50 replies a day
- Bonus: 1 promotion a day
- Sending 1 good post a day to network
- Consider paying for growth

Create a time-block for those in your mornings or whenever you have quiet time to focus.
