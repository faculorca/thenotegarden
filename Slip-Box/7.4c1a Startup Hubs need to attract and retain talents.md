# 7.4c1a Startup Hubs need to attract and retain talents

Startup hubs are even more than incubators, they are ecosystems of ambitious people.

This ecosystem is interesting to people who want to make big things, because there is where the big things happen.

The mission of any startup hub is bringing as much great people to that place to grow an exciting and synergistic place.
