# 3.1a Having control of your notes is rare in todays age

Today, infinite amount of note-taking systems exist. Few of them let you have control of your own notes.

When you start using proprietary software that don’t give you access to the raw files, your notes, your highlights and your thinking get stuck inside a company’s database. That data is not yours, you are renting the place and putting the input to give it to your software of preference.
