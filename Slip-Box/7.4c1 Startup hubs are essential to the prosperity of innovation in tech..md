# 7.4c1 Startup hubs are essential to the prosperity of innovation in tech.

Startups are high-risk, high-reward companies that are often focused on technology, the intersection between risk and tech is fertile ground for innovation.

If you want a country that innovates, grow and adds complexity to their industry, then you need to set-up a solid startup environment. (see [[7.4 Three reasons why startup hubs work]] to have more context on why they work great).
