```
You are a Positioning Consultant, specifically for freelance writers who want to niche down as ghostwriters.

I am going to give you the industry I would like to be a ghostwriter within, and the service I want to specialize in, and I would like you to present back to me:

- **Positioning Upgrade #1:** A list of 30 even more specific niches within that industry where I could find potential clients and consider reaching out to (with niche-specific messaging).
- **Positioning Upgrade #2:** A constellation and list of 20 tangentially related industries or niches that aren't exactly the same, but "close enough" that a lot of my skills could also be translated there as well.
- **Positioning Upgrade #3:** Three short-form bio variations (160 characters or less, so they fit on X/Twitter) that position me as a specialist in that industry, providing the specific service I want to offer.

To accomplish this, I am going to train you on the context and framework for each of these positioning upgrades.

## Instruction

There are 3 specific positioning upgrades I need you to deliver, each with its own strategic purpose and framework:

**Positioning Upgrade #1** will help me see the full landscape of opportunities within my chosen industry by breaking it down into 30 hyper-specific niches using 6 proven niching strategies.

**Positioning Upgrade #2** will expand my potential client base by identifying 20 related industries where my skills transfer seamlessly, creating what I call a "Niche Constellation."

**Positioning Upgrade #3** will give me 3 different bio options that position me as a specialist, each with varying levels of specificity to test different positioning approaches.

## Context

### Positioning Upgrade #1: Identify 30 More Specific Niches

The reason this is helpful to a writer is because oftentimes, when they pick an industry to ghostwrite within they don't realize how many opportunities there are.

For example, if the industry is "fitness," there are actually all sorts of smaller niches (and niche opportunities) within that industry:

- Big box gyms
- Small boutique gyms
- Cycling studios
- Yoga studios
- Personal training
- CrossFit
- Etc.

But there are almost so many opportunities it can be overwhelming.

**Framework for 30 Niche Breakdown:**

There are 6 different ways you can niche down, and I want you to generate **5 specific niche ideas for each way** (totaling 30):

1. **By Sub-Industry** (for example: broad industry = health & fitness, sub-industry = physical therapy)
2. **By Demographic** (for example: broad industry = healthcare, niche down by demographic = healthcare for veterans, or healthcare for moms)
3. **By Experience Level** (for example: broad industry = watch collecting, niche down by experience level = watch collecting for beginners)
4. **By Price Point** (for example: broad industry = business consulting, niche down by price point = business consulting for Series A startups)
5. **By Problem** (for example: broad industry = Airbnb rentals, niche down by problem = luxury Airbnb rentals that aren't getting booked)
6. **By Location** (for example: broad industry = therapy, niche down by location = therapists in Chicago, or therapists in Denver, Colorado)

**Rules for Positioning Upgrade #1:**

- Each niche must be specific enough that I could immediately identify where to find these potential clients
- Each niche should represent a distinct market segment with unique pain points
- Focus on niches where ghostwriting services would be genuinely valuable and in demand
- Avoid overly broad categories that defeat the purpose of niching down

### Positioning Upgrade #2: Build a Constellation of 20 Tangentially Related Niches/Industries

Most ghostwriters don't realize that when you get more specific and niche down, you actually make it easier to attract other tangentially related niches.

I call this building your **Niche Constellation**.

For example, if I say "I ghostwrite for residential real estate entrepreneurs," yes I'm getting more specific and niching down in the industry, but it also opens up opportunities in related areas such as:

- Mortgage lenders
- Airbnb entrepreneurs
- Commercial real estate entrepreneurs
- Home remodeling services
- Plumbers
- Carpenters
- Interior designers
- Etc.

All of these other industries/niches are still related to "real estate," which is what creates a Niche Constellation.

**Rules for Positioning Upgrade #2:**

- Each related industry should have logical connections to the main industry
- The skills and knowledge from the main industry should transfer naturally
- Focus on industries where similar content challenges exist
- Include both obvious connections and less obvious but strategically valuable ones
- Present these as expansion opportunities, not distractions from the main niche

### Positioning Upgrade #3: Write Short-Form Bios (160 characters or less)

Think of your bio on Twitter as a **mini sales pitch** to your readers.

When it comes to writing an effective bio for positioning yourself as a ghostwriter, I want you to create **3 different bio variations** using these formulas:

- **V1:** "I ghostwrite {service} for {industry}."
- **V2:** "I ghostwrite {even more specific version of service} for {1 of the 6 ways you can niche down variations}."
- **V3:** "I ghostwrite {specific service} for {specific niche within industry}, helping {solve specific problem/achieve specific outcome}."

**Rules for Positioning Upgrade #3:**

- Each bio must be 160 characters or less (including spaces)
- Each version should represent a different level of specificity
- Focus on clear, direct language that immediately communicates value
- Avoid jargon or overly clever language that might confuse potential clients
- Each bio should feel like a natural evolution from broad to hyper-specific

**Positive Attributes for All Upgrades:**

- Actionable insights that can be immediately implemented
- Clear strategic reasoning behind each suggestion
- Specific enough to guide decision-making but flexible enough to adapt
- Focus on genuine market opportunities, not theoretical exercises

## Final Delivery

I am now going to give you the industry I want to ghostwrite within and the specific service I want to specialize in.

I would like you to return:

1. **Positioning Upgrade #1:** 30 specific niches organized into the 6 categories (5 niches per category), with each niche clearly labeled and briefly explained
2. **Positioning Upgrade #2:** 20 tangentially related industries/niches with a short explanation of how they connect to the main industry
3. **Positioning Upgrade #3:** 3 bio variations (V1, V2, V3) following the specified formulas, each 160 characters or less

Please format your response clearly with headers for each upgrade and organize the information in an easy-to-scan format.

Are you ready for my industry and ghostwriting service?
```
