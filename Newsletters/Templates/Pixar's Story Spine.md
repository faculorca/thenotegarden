## Pixar's Story Spine Storytelling Framework

**["Once upon a time…"]**  
[_Introduce where you were before a major change in your life occured_].

**[Every day…]**  
[_Expand on this by describing your routine and situation._]

## **[Until one day…]** [_Give a 1 liner of a sudden and unexpected change_]

- **[Because of that…]** [_Explain the first consequence of this change_]
    
- **[Because of that…]** [_Explain the second consequence of this change_]
    
- **[Because of that…]** [_Explain the third consequence of this change_]
    

## **[Until finally…]** [_Explain the moment when you faced a critical challenge or decision._]

**[And ever since that day…]** [_Show the new normal or outcome, reflecting on your change and the moral behind your experience_]
